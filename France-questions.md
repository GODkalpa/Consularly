EMA Interview Questions
1. Course Name / Course Duration / Tuition Fees / Awarding Body
2. Describe your academic and professional background.
3. How does your academic and professional background align with the course that
you have chosen?
4. Describe your career objectives.
5. How will this course help you in achieving your career objectives?
6. Why did you choose this course in particular?
7. Why did you choose France?
8. Have you considered any other institutions other than EMA?
9. Have you previously applied/been refused a visa for France or any other
country?
10. Have you ever been to France? What do you know of France as a place?
11. Will you be looking to work while in France?
12. Who will be sponsoring you?
13. If you are unsuccessful in your application to France, what is your backup
plan?
14. How did you come to know about EMA?
15. Why did you choose EMA?

ICN Interview Questions 
1. First of all, tell us a bit about yourself. What is your academic background? 
(previous studies, current level of studies, field of studies) 
2. Why would you like to join ICN Business School and this programme ? 
3. Where do you see yourself in five years' time ? 
4. What is your main center of interest in life, and why? 
5. Which are the three main features of your personality that best describe you? 
6. Have you ever travelled abroad? If so, in which countries and on which 
occasions? 
7. Tell us about an experience in which you really invested yourself and of which 
you are particularly proud. 
8. What courses or activities at ICN Business School could nourish your        
ambitions? 
9. What are your motivations to join this programme? 
10. What are your career or professional plans, and how can the Bachelor 
programme of ICN help you to achieve your goal?
