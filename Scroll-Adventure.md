How It Works: Your Simple Path to Visa Interview Success
1. Create Your Account
Sign up in seconds at /signup using your email and password—or instantly with Google. Once registered, you’ll be guided to set up your profile.

2. Complete Your Profile
Tell us about your academic background and goals. On /profile-setup, select your degree level, program, university, duration of study, and estimated costs. This ensures all mock interviews are tailored just for you.

3. Access Your Dashboard
Head to /dashboard to view your interview quota, session history, and next steps. You’ll only see this page once you’re signed in and your profile is complete—no loose ends.

4. Start a Mock Interview
From your dashboard, pick your visa route (e.g., USA F-1 or UK Tier 4) and click Start Interview. A new window opens to check your camera and microphone; select Start and dive into your personalized simulation.

5. Review Results & Improve
Finish the session to receive detailed AI-driven scores and actionable feedback. Revisit all past interviews under My Results on your dashboard to track your progress and master every question.
